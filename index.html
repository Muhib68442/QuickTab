QuickTab is a lightweight and modern Chrome Extension that replaces your boring new tab with a smarter, faster, and customizable dashboard.

It’s designed to boost productivity and give you quick access to the things that matter most.

  • Aesthetic Themes – Make every new tab visually inspiring.
  • Smart Widgets – Calendar, Notes, To-Do list at your fingertips.
  • Ultimate Usefulness – Access what matters instantly.
  • Settings & Customization – Adjust colors, layout, and style your way.
  • Seamless Experience – Fast, lightweight, and distraction-free.

Setup Process

  • Download the .zip file and extract
  • Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions/
  • Enable "Developer Mode" in the top right corner
  • Click "Load unpacked" and browser the extracted folder
  • Browse the QuickTab folder and Click on Select Folder
  • On creating a new tab for the first time,
    click on 'Keep It' to apply as default new tab
  • Done ! Enjoy your Aesthetic New Tab
